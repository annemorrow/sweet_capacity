Sweet Capacity is a play on heat capacity the extensive property of a material which determines the temperature change observed when heat energy is added or removed. Similarly, “sweet capacity” describes the property of increased human happiness with each subsequent morsel of delicious chocolate.

Like many of you reading this, Zak’s journey into the world of chocolate began by searching for more and better chocolate. He accidentally stumbled upon Barry Callebaut’s Chocolate Academy, attended, and realized that his background in chemistry could be APPLIED to the practical problem of increasing confections’ Sweet Capacity.

Sweet Capacity, LLC, has three distinct goals:

  1. Create original, fun, sweet-tasting products for your enjoyment.
  2. Encourage sustainability and ethical cacao (chocolate) production.
  3. Provide resources to those interested in learning more about the chemistry and physics of chocolate.
